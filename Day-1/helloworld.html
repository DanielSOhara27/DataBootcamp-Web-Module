How to make a sandwich

  1. Get 2 slices of sandwich bread
  2. Spread mayonnaise on one side of one piece of bread
  3. Spread mustard on one side of a second piece of bread that is different from the one with mayonnaise
  4. Place 2 slices of ham on top of the bread with the mayonnaise (on the same side as the mayonnaise)
  5. Place a slice of lettuce, tomato and onion on top of the ham
  6. Place the slice of bread with mustard on top of the lettuce, tomato & onion with the mustard facing the vegetables
